Shobhita Sundaram

Hi! I'm a senior at MIT studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I am primarily interested in computer vision, particularly representation learning, learning from synthetic data, and 3D perception. I'm also interested in using insights from neuroscience and cognitive science to build better artificial intelligence systems.

I work at the MIT Center for Brains, Minds, and Machines with Pawan Sinha and Xavier Boix. In the past I've been fortunate to work at D. E. Shaw (quantitative research), Apple (machine learning), and Two Sigma (software engineering). In my free time I enjoy hiking, writing, and running.
